    About X-Crawler

    X-Crawler is a Publication Crawling project, used to search for research papers, publications written by the staff of the University of Lagos. This project was part of the problems presented by the DSC (Developer Student Club), University of Lagos at The Open Hack Week Hackathon. During the course of this project different technologies where used and different Academic Websites were scraped for the possibility of the Project. We have a bunch of people who worked on this project, and without them this project would not be possible.
